Sedona is the forest oasis that is located in Northern Arizona, and home to these fantastic rock formations that have been told to have spiritual healing powers.

These Vortexes and amazing sights do well to compliment the quaint little down town. Filled with shops and handcrafted goods, these draw people from all over.
